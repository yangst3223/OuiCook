Ingredient List

2 cups cooked rice
2 eggs
2 tablespoons soy sauce
Some sliced pork for added protein

Nutritional Information

Total Calories: 500 kcal
Protein: 20g
Carbohydrates: 60g
Fat: 15g

Fried Rice

Fried rice is a classic Asian dish.
Elevated by high-heat stir-frying, which enhances the flavor of rice coated in soy sauce and scrambled eggs It’s a dish that uses simple ingredients yet challenges the cook’s culinary skills
The rich flavor of fried rice comes from the high heat of the wok
Creating the distinctive “Breath of a Wok”
Using day-old rice helps each grain remain separate
Giving the fried rice a perfect texture

Cooking Steps

1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat (no need to add oil).
Add the sliced pork to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until fully cooked through.

2. Push the pork to one side of the skillet and pour in the beaten eggs.
Stir continuously to scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked.

3. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, stirring to combine with the pork and eggs.
Pour the soy sauce over the mixture, stirring well to evenly coat the rice.

4. Continue to stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
Serve hot and enjoy your simple fried rice with egg and pork !
